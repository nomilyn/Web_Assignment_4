Volcano Images

Mount Mayon Mount Taal Mount Pinatubo

These are the three most active volcanoes in the Philippines. Mount Mayon is a classic stratovolcano with a small central summit crater. The cone is considered the world's most perfectly formed volcano for its symmetry. Mount Taal is a beautiful caldera volcano, but one of the most active and dangerous volcanoes in the Philippines. This is famous and one of the country's most popular tourist destinations because of its breathtaking sceneray at Taal Lake. Pinatubo is most notorious for its VEI-6 eruption on June 15, 1991, the second-largest terrestrial eruption of the 20th century after the 1912 eruption of Novarupta in Alaska.
